Protecting the Earth for future generations

What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term changes in temperature, weather patterns, and environmental conditions caused mainly by human activities such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and pollution.

Climate Change Pictures

Effects of Climate Change

  • Rising temperatures across the world
  • Floods, droughts, and stronger storms
  • Melting glaciers and rising sea levels
  • Loss of wildlife and natural habitats
  • Health problems caused by pollution and heat

Ways to Reduce Climate Change

  • Plant more trees and protect forests
  • Use renewable energy like solar and wind
  • Reduce plastic waste and recycle materials
  • Save electricity and water at home
  • Use public transport, bicycles, or walk more often
